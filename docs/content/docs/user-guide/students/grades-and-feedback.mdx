# Grades and Feedback

## Checking Assignment Status

The assignment page usually shows a status panel.

You may see:

- `Not submitted`
- `Submitted`
- `Graded`

You may also see:

- submitted time
- last edited time
- due date

Use this panel to confirm Leopard recorded your submission correctly.

## How Grades Work

Leopard separates grading into two steps:

1. the teacher saves the grade
2. the teacher publishes the grade

Students only see grades and feedback after the teacher publishes them.

So if your teacher says grading is done but you still do not see your result, they may not have published grades yet.

## What You Can See After Grades Are Released

Once released, the assignment page may show:

- your grade
- written feedback
- when the work was graded

## Student Workflow Example

1. Sign in.
2. Join the correct class.
3. Open an assignment.
4. Launch the workspace.
5. Complete and submit the work.
6. Return later to check grades and feedback.

## Student Tips

- If your grade is missing, ask whether grades have been published.
- Check the assignment page itself, not just the classroom page.
- Read the teacher's feedback carefully before moving on.
