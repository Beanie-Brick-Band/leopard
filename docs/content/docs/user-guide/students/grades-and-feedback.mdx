## How Grades Work

Leopard separates grading into two steps:

1. the teacher saves the grade and feedback
2. the teacher publishes the result

Students only see grades and feedback after the teacher publishes them.

So if your teacher says grading is complete but you still do not see a score, they may not have published grades yet.

## Where To Look

- `Your Grade` shows the released score.
- The same card shows written feedback from the teacher.
- `Assignment Status` keeps the submitted time and due date in view.

## If You Still Do Not See A Grade

- Reopen the assignment page directly instead of checking only the classroom list.
- Ask whether grades were published yet.
- Confirm you are looking at the assignment you actually submitted.

## Student Tips

- Read the feedback before starting the next assignment.
- Keep the assignment page open while you compare the comments with your final submission.
- If something looks wrong, contact the teacher from the assignment page context so you are discussing the correct submission.
